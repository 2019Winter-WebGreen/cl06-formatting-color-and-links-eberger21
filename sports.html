About Sports and why everyone should play one

Sports are beneficial to everyone's health. They help people mentally grow by teaching teamwork, leadership, and communication. They help people physcially grow by strenghtening bones and muscles as well as contributing to the loss of body fat.

There's also so many different types of sports depending on how much contact you want. Some sports are:

  • Soccer
  • Basketball
  • Football
  • Baseball
  • Tennis
  • Hockey

There are so many benefits to sports including that they even make you smarter! But they even make you happier if you choose one that you love to do! Choose a sport today and change your life!
